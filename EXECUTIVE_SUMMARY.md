## 🎯 The Problem

Customer support teams waste hours manually:

- **Triaging emails** (priority and category classification)
- **Drafting responses** to customer complaints
- **Resolving multi-email threads** with conflicting information
- **Detecting fraud** and phishing attempts

This is expensive, slow, and error-prone. **Email Triage OpenEnv** demonstrates how AI can automate this workflow end-to-end.

---

## ✨ The Solution

A production-ready AI training environment with:

### ✅ **5 Realistic Tasks**

1. **Classify** emails (easy)
2. **Draft responses** (medium)
3. **Resolve threads** (hard)
4. **Investigate issues** (medium-hard)
5. **Full workflow** (hardest)

### ✅ **Deterministic Grading**

- Partial credit for partial success
- Reproducible results for research
- 25+ grading tests validating fairness

### ✅ **OpenEnv Compliant**

- Standard HTTP interface
- Proper environment variables
- Structured logging format

### 🚀 **8 Innovative Features**

1. **Performance Dashboard** - Real-time learning curves with anomaly detection
2. **Multi-Agent Benchmarking** - Compare agents side-by-side with rankings
3. **Explainability Reports** - Understand why each decision was scored
4. **Business Impact Metrics** - Quantify ROI in USD
5. **Adversarial Testing** - Verify production readiness
6. **Threat Intelligence** - Enterprise-grade phishing detection
7. **Transfer Learning** - Track skill acquisition and reuse
8. **Curriculum Optimization** - Personalized learning paths
